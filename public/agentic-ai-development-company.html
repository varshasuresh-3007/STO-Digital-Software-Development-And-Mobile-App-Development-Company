What Is Agentic AI?

The AI systems with autonomous agents that have the ability to:

🎯
Setting and pursuing goals
🗺️
Planning multi-step actions
🛠️
Engaging with systems, APIs and tools.
📈
Feedback and experience learning.
🤝
Work with other agents and human beings.

In contrast to classical AI or rule-based automation, agentic AI systems are active, dynamic, and autonomous. They are best suited to complex business scenarios where dynamic and scale-based decisions need to be made.
